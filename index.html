Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredient Quantity
salted butter (softened) 1 cup
white sugar (granulated) 1 cup
chocolate (chopped or chips) 2 cups
light brown sugar packed 1 cup
vanilla extract 2 tsp
large eggs 2
all-purpose flour 3 cups
baking soda 1 tsp
baking powder 1/2 tsp
sea salt 1 tsp

Instructions

  • Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl and set aside for later: flour, sea salt, baking bowder, and baking soda.
  • In a seperate bowl, whisk together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until combined.
  • Next, add the eggs to the butter and sugar and whisk until fluffy. Add in the vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  • Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  • Add chocolate chips and fold into the dough.
  • Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper or spread butter to the tray to avoid the cookies sticking to its surface.
  • Preheat the oven to 370 degrees Farenheight
  • Bake for 7-9 minutes or until a light golden brow color begins to appear on the cookies.
  • Take out of the oven and let it cool for 5 minutes.

Vegan Banana Bread

Ingredient Quantity
flax seed powder 2 tbsp
coconut oil 1/2 cup
mayple syrup or honey (not vegan) 1/3 cup
almond milk 1/4 cup
vanilla extract 2 tsp
flour - all purpose or whole wheat 1 3/4 cup
cinnamon 1/3 tsp
baking soda 1 tsp
salt 1/2 tsp
toppings - optional (walnuts, rainsins, chocolate chips) 1/3 cup

Instructions

  • Prepare the flax egg - mix the flax seed powder with 6 tbsp of water. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  • Mix together the dry ingredients in a bowl - flour, cinnamon, salt, and baking soda
  • In a separate bowl, mix together mayple syrup or honey with coconut oil and almond milk until combined.
  • Add the flax egg to the wet ingredients and whisk until combined. Add vanilla extract and stir.
  • Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  • Add topings and fold into the mixture (optional).
  • Prepare a baking tray by spraying coconut oil to the tray to avoid the cake sticking to its surface.
  • Preheat the oven to 325 degrees Farenheight
  • Bake for 50-55 minutes.
  • Take out of the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes.
