Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar (granulated)
  • 2 cups chocolate (chopped or chips)
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Instructions

  • Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl and set aside for later: flour, sea salt, baking bowder, and baking soda.
  • In a seperate bowl, whisk together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until combined.
  • Next, add the eggs to the butter and sugar and whisk until fluffy. Add in the vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  • Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  • Add chocolate chips and fold into the dough.
  • Prepare a baking tray with parchment paper or spread butter to the tray to avoid the cookies sticking to its surface.
  • Preheat the oven to 370 degrees Farenheight
  • Bake for 7-9 minutes or until a light golden brow color begins to appear on the cookies.
  • Take out of the oven and let it cool for 5 minutes.
